Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking, requirements, and policy before you VentureOut.

How do I secure a spot for a travel package? +

Simply navigate to our booking page, fill out the enquiry form with your contact details and desired destination, and click submit. One of our destination planners will email or call you within 24 hours to finalize your flight choices, dates, and process payments.

What is your cancellation and refund policy? +

We offer 100% full refunds for cancellations made up to 30 days prior to the departure date. Cancellations made between 14 to 29 days are eligible for a 50% refund, or a full credit toward any future bookings within 12 months.

Are international airline flights included? +

Our baseline package prices cover all local ground transport, boutique accommodations, select guides, and excursions. International flights are not included in the standard price, but we offer cheap flight add-on rates through our carrier partnerships during consultation.

Do you organize customized private group tours? +

Absolutely! If you are planning a corporate retreat, family reunion, or group wedding, we design custom itineraries with dedicated private guides, private vans, and personalized dining menus tailored to your preferences.
